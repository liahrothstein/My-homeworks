20 chocolate round wafers, divided

Directions
ARRANGE 8 to 9 ice cream sandwiches in 9-inch square pan, cutting to
form an even layer. Spread with half of whipped topping. Break or crush
chocolate wafers and sprinkle over the whipped cream. Microwave 1
cup of chocolate topping into small microwave-safe bowl on HIGH for
30 seconds. Drizzle evenly over wafers.
TOP with remaining 8 to 9 ice cream sandwiches to form an even layer.
Spread with remaining whipped topping. Freeze 3 hours or overnight.
